What we offer

Designed for professional and new property investors alike, SIBON is a tool to help you assess and manage buy-to-let (BTL) properties in an innovative way.

Manage your properties Coming Soon

Track the rental income and expenses of your properties in one place. SIBON can prompt you to think about your next move – before you end up spending way more than you wanted to on a property. Maybe the spend is all part of your genius investment plan, in which case you can ignore, breathe and carry on!

Tailored notifications Coming Soon

Save the properties that have caught your attention and we’ll notify you when there's been a price change in any of your saved properties. Set your preferences and we can also notify you when there are other properties that meet your preferences, be it by location or preferred yield. Now that’s handy!

Calcastor Coming Soon

Britain’s most powerful tool for calculating and forecasting property earnings. Additional features to come include comparing multiple properties, stress-testing different economic-driven scenarios and more customisable bits and bobs.

Dashboard Coming Soon

Where it all happens! Set your preferences, analyse your personalised stats, and save the potential properties you want to keep your eyes on. Our ask? Use this space to help us help you!
